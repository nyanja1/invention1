Comprehensive Logistics Solutions

At Inbound Holdings East Africa Ltd, we provide end-to-end logistics and supply chain solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of businesses across various industries. Our comprehensive range of services ensures efficient movement and management of goods throughout the supply chain.

Our team of experienced professionals utilizes advanced technology and industry best practices to deliver reliable, cost-effective solutions that optimize your logistics operations and drive business growth.

Freight Transportation

Our comprehensive freight transportation services cover road, rail, air, and sea freight, providing flexible solutions for moving goods across East Africa and beyond.

  • Road Freight: Full and partial load services
  • Air Freight: Express and standard air cargo services
  • Sea Freight: FCL and LCL container shipping
  • Rail Freight: Cost-effective transport for bulk goods
  • Multimodal Transportation: Combined transport solutions

Warehousing & Distribution

Our modern warehousing facilities offer secure storage with advanced inventory management systems to ensure efficient product handling and distribution.

  • General and Specialized Storage Solutions
  • Inventory Management and Control
  • Order Fulfillment and Distribution
  • Cross-Docking Services
  • Value-Added Services (Packaging, Labeling, etc.)

Customs Clearance

Our customs clearance services ensure smooth border transitions with efficient documentation processing and compliance with regulatory requirements.

  • Import and Export Customs Documentation
  • Customs Compliance Consulting
  • Duty and Tax Management
  • Specialized Permits and Licenses
  • Border Clearance Facilitation

Supply Chain Solutions

Our integrated supply chain solutions optimize your entire logistics network, improving efficiency and reducing costs while ensuring reliability.

  • Supply Chain Consulting and Optimization
  • Integrated Logistics Management
  • Demand Planning and Forecasting
  • 4PL Services and Control Tower Operations

Project Logistics
